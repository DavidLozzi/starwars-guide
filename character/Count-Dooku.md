    <p><p>Count Dooku, also known by his Sith name Darth Tyranus, is a human noble from Serenno who rose to become a respected Jedi Master before secretly embracing the dark side. He is best known for abandoning the Jedi Order, serving as the apprentice of Darth Sidious, and founding the Confederacy of Independent Systems, which sparked the Clone Wars. Major milestones in his life include his Jedi training and mastery, resignation to pursue Sith ambitions, orchestration of the Separatist movement, leading key battles such as Geonosis, and ultimately his demise at the hands of Anakin Skywalker during the war.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>~102 BBY - Birth</h3><p>Dooku is born into Serenno's noble Dooku family, destined for a life of privilege and leadership.</p><h3>~90 BBY - Jedi Initiate</h3><p>At a young age, Dooku is taken to the Jedi Temple and begins his training under the tutelage of several Masters.</p><h3>~67 BBY - Jedi Knight</h3><p>Completes his training and is granted the rank of Jedi Knight, demonstrating exceptional skill and leadership.</p><h3>~52 BBY - Jedi Master</h3><p>Elevated to Jedi Master, Dooku serves on the Jedi High Council and mentors numerous Padawans.</p><h3>~44 BBY - Resignation from Jedi Order</h3><p>Disillusioned by corruption in the Republic and the Jedi Order’s role, Dooku renounces his rank and departs the Order.</p><h3>~44 BBY - Fall to Sith (Darth Tyranus)</h3><p>Secretly taken as the apprentice of Darth Sidious, he adopts the Sith name Darth Tyranus and begins executing Sith plans.</p><h3>~32 BBY - Formation of the Confederacy of Independent Systems</h3><p>Under his leadership, dozens of star systems secede from the Republic, forming the Separatist movement.</p><h3>~22 BBY - Battle of Geonosis</h3><p>Leads Separatist forces in the first open conflict of the Clone Wars and duels with former apprentice Obi-Wan Kenobi.</p><h3>~22 to ~19 BBY - Clone Wars</h3><p>Serves as political and military head of the Separatists, orchestrating major campaigns and confrontations across the galaxy.</p><h3>~19 BBY - Death</h3><p>Onboard the Separatist flagship Invisible Hand, Dooku is challenged and slain by Anakin Skywalker at the behest of his Sith master.</p></div>

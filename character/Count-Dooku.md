    <p>
    Count Dooku (Darth Tyranus)     was born in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Count%20Dooku?year=-102" target="_blank">102 BBY</a> and died in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Count%20Dooku?year=-19" target="_blank">19 BBY</a>.        
    </p>

    <p><p>Count Dooku, also known by his Sith title Darth Tyranus, was a Force-sensitive human male born into the noble ruling family of Serenno. Trained as a Jedi by Grand Master Yoda, he became one of the Order's most respected swordmasters and lightsaber duelists, but grew disillusioned with the Jedi's subservience to a Republic he saw as corrupt. Following the death of his former Padawan Qui-Gon Jinn, Dooku left the Jedi Order, reclaimed his hereditary title of Count of Serenno, and secretly became the second Sith apprentice of Darth Sidious. As the public leader and Head of State of the Confederacy of Independent Systems, he ignited and led the Separatist cause during the Clone Wars, orchestrating the conflict as part of Sidious's plan to destroy the Jedi and seize control of the galaxy. He trained dark side agents such as Asajj Ventress and Savage Opress and dueled the galaxy's greatest Jedi. Ultimately, Dooku was betrayed by his own master: during the Battle of Coruscant he was defeated and executed by Anakin Skywalker at Sidious's command, a casualty of the very Sith treachery he had embraced.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>102 BBY - Birth on Serenno</h3><p>Dooku is born into the wealthy noble House of Serenno, heir to the title of Count. As a Force-sensitive infant he is given over to the Jedi Order and taken to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant.</p><h3>~92 BBY - Training under Yoda</h3><p>As a youngling and later Padawan, Dooku is trained in the ways of the Force by Grand Master Yoda, growing into one of the Order's most skilled and respected members and a master of lightsaber combat.</p><h3>~44 BBY - Master to Qui-Gon Jinn</h3><p>Dooku takes Qui-Gon Jinn as his Padawan, forging a bond that would later shape his disillusionment with the Jedi and the Republic.</p><h3>32 BBY - Departure from the Jedi Order</h3><p>The death of his former apprentice Qui-Gon Jinn at the Battle of Naboo deepens Dooku's disillusionment. He leaves the Jedi Order and reclaims his hereditary title as Count of Serenno.</p><h3>~32 BBY - Becoming Darth Tyranus</h3><p>Dooku is taken as the second apprentice of the Sith Lord Darth Sidious, adopting the identity Darth Tyranus. On Sidious's orders he arranges for the creation of a secret clone army on Kamino under the name of the late Jedi Sifo-Dyas.</p><h3>~24 BBY - Rise of the Separatist movement</h3><p>Dooku publicly emerges as the charismatic leader of a growing secessionist movement, rallying thousands of star systems in opposition to the Galactic Republic.</p><h3>22 BBY - The Battle of Geonosis</h3><p>Dooku's Separatist plans are exposed on Geonosis. He captures Obi-Wan Kenobi, duels Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker, and Yoda, and escapes as the first battle of the Clone Wars erupts, delivering the Death Star plans to Sidious.</p><h3>22-19 BBY - Leading the Confederacy in the Clone Wars</h3><p>As Head of State of the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Dooku directs the Separatist war effort, trains dark side agents such as Asajj Ventress and Savage Opress, and repeatedly clashes with Jedi across the galaxy.</p><h3>19 BBY - Death at the Battle of Coruscant</h3><p>During the Separatist assault on Coruscant, Dooku duels Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi aboard the flagship Invisible Hand. Anakin overpowers and disarms him, then decapitates him at Palpatine's command—revealing Dooku had been a disposable pawn in his own master's scheme.</p></div>

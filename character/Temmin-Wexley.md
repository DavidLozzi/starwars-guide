    Temmin Wexley (Snap)     was born in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Temmin%20Wexley?year=-11" target="_blank">11 BBY</a> and died in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Temmin%20Wexley?year=35" target="_blank">35 ABY</a>.        
    </p>

    <p><p>Temmin "Snap" Wexley was a human male pilot born in the city of Myrra on the planet Akiva. As a child he was separated from his parents when his father Brentin was seized by the Galactic Empire and his mother Norra left to fight for the Rebel Alliance, leaving him in the care of his aunt. A gifted mechanic, he became an independent junk dealer and built a modified B1 battle droid, Mister Bones, as a bodyguard and companion. After the Battle of Endor he reunited with his mother and helped foil a secret Imperial gathering on Akiva. He went on to train as a pilot, gained Wedge Antilles as a stepfather, and flew at the Battle of Jakku. Decades later he became a founding pilot of the Resistance, serving in Black Squadron under Poe Dameron and marrying fellow pilot Karé Kun. He fought at the destruction of Starkiller Base and was ultimately shot down and killed during the Battle of Exegol.</p></p>

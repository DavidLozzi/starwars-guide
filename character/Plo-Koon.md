    <p>"<p>Plo Koon was a male Kel Dor Jedi Master from the planet Dorin who served on the Jedi High Council through the final decades of the Galactic Republic. Renowned for his level-headed judgment, compassion and unflappable calm, he wore an antiox breath mask and protective goggles to survive in oxygen-rich atmospheres, and he was a gifted pilot and swordsman who also commanded the rare Force technique known as Electric Judgment. He sat on the Council during the Naboo Crisis and fought at the First Battle of Geonosis that opened the Clone Wars, then served as a Jedi General leading the 104th Battalion — the \"Wolfpack\" — alongside Clone Commander Wolffe, with whom he shared an unusually close and respectful bond, insisting that clones be treated as men rather than materiel. Among his most enduring legacies was discovering the Force-sensitive Togruta child Ahsoka Tano on Shili and bringing her to the Jedi Temple, remaining a mentor and protector to her for the rest of his life. He survived the destruction of his fleet by General Grievous's warship Malevolence, took part in the rescue of Even Piell from the Citadel, and fought across countless campaigns before Order 66 in 19 BBY, when the clone pilots escorting him over the bridge cities of Cato Neimoidia turned on their general and shot down his starfighter, killing him instantly.</p>"</p>

    <div class="timeline">"<h3>74 BBY - Birth on Dorin</h3><p>Plo Koon is born a member of the Kel Dor species on Dorin, a world whose helium- and gas-rich atmosphere means he must wear an antiox mask and goggles anywhere else in the galaxy.</p><h3>~36 BBY - Finding Ahsoka Tano on Shili</h3><p>Sent by the Jedi Order to investigate a powerful Force signature, Plo Koon locates the infant Togruta Ahsoka Tano in the wilds of Shili and brings her to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, beginning a lifelong bond as her protector.</p><h3>32 BBY - The Naboo Crisis</h3><p>Now a member of the Jedi High Council, Plo Koon is present when Qui-Gon Jinn presents Anakin Skywalker for testing and when the Council debates the return of the Sith after the Battle of Naboo.</p><h3>22 BBY - The First Battle of Geonosis</h3><p>Plo Koon joins the two-hundred-strong Jedi strike team that descends on Geonosis to rescue Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker and Padme Amidala, surviving the arena battle that ignites the Clone Wars.</p><h3>22 BBY - General of the 104th Battalion</h3><p>Given command of the clone troopers of the 104th Battalion, nicknamed the Wolfpack, Plo Koon forges a deep partnership with Commander Wolffe and insists his men be regarded as individuals rather than expendable soldiers.</p><h3>22 BBY - Destruction of his fleet by the Malevolence</h3><p>Plo Koon's task force is annihilated by General Grievous's ion-cannon warship Malevolence near Abregado; adrift in an escape pod with a handful of survivors including Wolffe, he is rescued by Anakin Skywalker and Ahsoka Tano and helps expose the weapon's nature.</p><h3>~22 BBY - Defense of Kamino</h3><p>Alongside Obi-Wan Kenobi, Plo Koon helps anticipate and repel a Separatist assault on the cloning facilities of Kamino led by Grievous and Asajj Ventress.</p><h3>~21 BBY - The Citadel</h3><p>Plo Koon takes part in the Republic operation to extract Jedi Master Even Piell from the Separatist prison fortress on Lola Sayu, commanding the relief force that recovers the survivors.</p><h3>22-19 BBY - Campaigns of the Clone Wars</h3><p>Across the war Plo Koon leads front-line actions from Felucia to the Outer Rim while continuing to serve on the Jedi High Council, and stands among the Masters who wrestle with Ahsoka Tano's expulsion and vindication.</p><h3>19 BBY - Death over Cato Neimoidia</h3><p>While patrolling the bridge cities of Cato Neimoidia in his Jedi starfighter, Plo Koon is caught by Order 66 when the clone pilots of his own escort open fire; his fighter is destroyed and he is killed in the crash, one of the first Jedi to fall in the purge.</p>"</div>

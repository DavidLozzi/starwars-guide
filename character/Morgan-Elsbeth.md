    <p><p>Morgan Elsbeth was a Force-sensitive human female Nightsister of Dathomir who rose from the ashes of her clan's destruction to become one of the Galactic Empire's most important shipwrights and, later, the driving force behind Grand Admiral Thrawn's return from exile. Born on Dathomir before the Clone Wars, she survived the massacre of the Nightsisters — in which her mother was killed — and spent years as a wanderer and survivor, allying briefly with the Mountain Clan before her ruthlessness and talent for industry brought her into the Empire's service, where she helped construct the Imperial Navy and became a devoted follower of Thrawn. By the New Republic era she ruled the walled city of Calodan on Corvus as its magistrate, terrorizing its people with a regime that wore the chimaera emblem of Thrawn's 7th Fleet; there she offered a spear of pure beskar to the Mandalorian Din Djarin as payment to kill the former Jedi Ahsoka Tano, only to be defeated by Tano herself. Freed from New Republic custody by the mercenaries Baylan Skoll and Shin Hati, Elsbeth pursued a map to a distant galaxy, built the hyperspace ring <i>Eye of Sion</i>, and reached the world of Peridea, where the Great Mothers formally accepted her into the Nightsister order and granted her the gift of shadows and the Blade of Talzin. Fighting to buy Thrawn time to escape back to the known galaxy, she was struck down by Ahsoka Tano, dying with the hope of restoring Dathomir's culture unfulfilled.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>Before the Clone Wars - Birth on Dathomir</h3> <p>Morgan Elsbeth was born on Dathomir into the Nightsister clan sometime before the Clone Wars. Canon gives no year for her birth.</p> <h3>~19 BBY - Survivor of the Nightsister Massacre</h3> <p>Elsbeth witnessed the destruction of her people during the Separatist assault on Dathomir, in which her mother was killed. She survived alone amid the ruins of her clan.</p> <h3>~19 BBY - Wandering and the Mountain Clan</h3> <p>Left without a home, Elsbeth wandered Dathomir and eventually fell in with the Mountain Clan, who took her in before she turned on those who wronged her.</p> <h3>Reign of the Empire - Imperial Shipwright</h3> <p>Elsbeth's skill as a starship designer and industrialist made her instrumental in the construction of the Imperial Navy, and she became a dedicated follower of Grand Admiral Thrawn, whose chimaera emblem her forces later wore.</p> <h3>9 ABY - Magistrate of Calodan</h3> <p>By the New Republic era, Elsbeth ruled the walled city of Calodan on the planet Corvus, stripping the world bare and terrorizing its citizens with her guards and the mercenary Lang.</p> <h3>9 ABY - The Beskar Spear</h3> <p>She hired the Mandalorian Din Djarin, offering a spear of pure beskar as payment to kill the former Jedi Ahsoka Tano, who had come to Corvus hunting information about Thrawn.</p> <h3>9 ABY - Defeat on Corvus</h3> <p>Ahsoka Tano liberated Calodan and defeated Elsbeth in single combat, demanding to know the location of Grand Admiral Thrawn before taking her into custody.</p> <h3>9 ABY - Rescued by Baylan Skoll and Shin Hati</h3> <p>While being transported by the New Republic, Elsbeth was freed by the mercenaries Baylan Skoll and Shin Hati, whom she had employed, and resumed her search for Thrawn.</p> <h3>9 ABY - The Star Map and the Eye of Sion</h3> <p>Elsbeth recovered an ancient star map pointing to a distant galaxy and oversaw construction of the enormous hyperspace ring known as the Eye of Sion above Seatos.</p> <h3>9 ABY - Journey to Peridea</h3> <p>After a confrontation with Ahsoka Tano at Seatos, Elsbeth launched the Eye of Sion and crossed to Peridea, the ancestral world of the Dathomiri witches, where she found Thrawn in exile.</p> <h3>9 ABY - Accepted as a Nightsister</h3> <p>The Great Mothers formally inducted Elsbeth into the Nightsister order, granting her the gift of shadows and the Blade of Talzin.</p> <h3>9 ABY - Death on Peridea</h3> <p>During the Battle of Peridea, Elsbeth stayed behind at the Great Mothers' fortress to hold off Ahsoka Tano and allow Thrawn to escape. Declaring her sacrifice was for Dathomir rather than the Empire, she was killed by Tano.</p></div>

    <p><p>Hera Syndulla is a Twi'lek revolutionary leader and ace pilot who became one of the founding figures of the rebellion against the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Born on Ryloth to the resistance leader General Cham Syndulla and Eleni Syndulla, she witnessed the devastation of the Clone Wars and Imperial occupation firsthand before leaving her homeworld to build her own fight across the galaxy aboard her starship, the Ghost. As commander of the rebel cell known as the Spectres, she led a crew that included the fallen Jedi Kanan Jarrus (who became her partner and the father of her son), the Mandalorian Sabine Wren, the Lasat Garazeb Orrelios, the astromech droid Chopper, and the Force-sensitive Padawan Ezra Bridger. She played key roles in the early insurgency on Lothal, the Battle of Atollon, and the liberation of Lothal, rising to the rank of general. Following the Empire's fall she continued to serve as a general in the New Republic, raising her son Jacen and searching for the missing Ezra Bridger, events central to the Ahsoka series.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>29 BBY - Birth on Ryloth</h3><p>Hera Syndulla is born on the Twi'lek homeworld of Ryloth to Cham Syndulla, a freedom fighter, and his wife Eleni.</p><h3>~19 BBY - Growing up amid the Clone Wars</h3><p>As a child during the Clone Wars, Hera experiences the Separatist occupation of Ryloth and her father's resistance movement, developing her lifelong drive to fight oppression and forming a bond with the droid Chopper.</p><h3>~11 BBY - Meeting Kanan Jarrus</h3><p>During the Gorse conflict, Hera crosses paths with Kanan Jarrus, a Jedi who survived Order 66; he later joins her crew aboard the Ghost and becomes her partner.</p><h3>~5 BBY - The Spectres on Lothal</h3><p>Leading her rebel cell from the Ghost, Hera recruits the street orphan Ezra Bridger and unites her crew — Kanan, Sabine Wren, Zeb Orrelios, and Chopper — into an insurgency against the Empire on Lothal.</p><h3>~4 BBY - Joining the wider rebellion</h3><p>Hera's cell links up with the growing rebel network under Phoenix Squadron and Commander Sato, and she reunites with her father Cham during a mission on Ryloth.</p><h3>~3 BBY - Battle of Atollon</h3><p>Hera fights in the disastrous Battle of Atollon against Grand Admiral Thrawn's forces, escaping with a portion of the rebel fleet.</p><h3>~2 BBY - Promotion and the Liberation of Lothal</h3><p>Now a rebel general, Hera leads the campaign to free Lothal. During the fighting Kanan Jarrus sacrifices himself to save her and the crew, and Ezra Bridger vanishes into hyperspace with Thrawn.</p><h3>~1 BBY - Birth of Jacen Syndulla</h3><p>Hera gives birth to her and Kanan's son, Jacen Syndulla, who later shows an aptitude for the Force.</p><h3>0 BBY–0 ABY - Galactic Civil War</h3><p>Hera continues to serve the Alliance to Restore the Republic as a pilot and general through the era of the Battle of Yavin.</p><h3>4 ABY - Battle of Endor</h3><p>General Syndulla participates in the Rebel Alliance's decisive victory over the Empire at Endor.</p><h3>~9 ABY - New Republic general</h3><p>Serving as a general in the New Republic, Hera raises Jacen, works to convince the Senate of the Imperial remnant and Thrawn threat, and joins Ahsoka Tano and Sabine Wren in the search for the missing Ezra Bridger during the events of the Ahsoka series.</p></div>

    Jabba Desilijic Tiure (the Hutt)     was born in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Jabba%20Desilijic%20Tiure?year=-300" target="_blank">600 BBY</a> and died in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Jabba%20Desilijic%20Tiure?year=4" target="_blank">4 ABY</a>.        
    </p>

    <p><p>Jabba Desilijic Tiure, better known as Jabba the Hutt, was a Hutt gangster and crime lord who ran a vast criminal empire from his palace on the Outer Rim desert world of Tatooine. A member of the powerful Grand Hutt Council and the Desilijic clan, Jabba controlled much of the galaxy's piracy, slaving, spice-running, and smuggling operations, employing bounty hunters and enforcers to protect his interests. He is remembered for sponsoring the Boonta Eve podrace, for his dealings with smugglers such as Han Solo during the Galactic Civil War, and for holding both Han Solo (frozen in carbonite) and Princess Leia Organa captive. He met his end during the rescue of Han Solo, when Leia strangled him with the very chain he had used to enslave her aboard his sail barge over the Great Pit of Carkoon.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>600 BBY - Birth</h3><p>Jabba Desilijic Tiure is born into the influential Desilijic clan of Hutts, a species originating from Nal Hutta.</p><h3>~32 BBY - Sponsoring the Boonta Eve Classic</h3><p>An established crime lord on Tatooine, Jabba presides over the Boonta Eve podrace won by the young Anakin Skywalker, underscoring his dominance over the desert world.</p><h3>22 BBY - The rescue of his son Rotta</h3><p>During the Clone Wars, Jabba's son Rotta is kidnapped in a Separatist plot. Anakin Skywalker and Ahsoka Tano recover the Huttlet, and a grateful Jabba grants the Republic passage through Hutt-controlled space.</p><h3>~22-19 BBY - Power during the Clone Wars</h3><p>Jabba wields influence from the Grand Hutt Council and weathers threats to his family and holdings, including the treachery of Ziro the Hutt.</p><h3>~1 BBY - Bounty on Han Solo</h3><p>After the smuggler Han Solo jettisons a spice shipment to avoid capture, Jabba places a bounty on his head, sending bounty hunters after him.</p><h3>0 BBY - Operations at the time of the Battle of Yavin</h3><p>Jabba continues to run his smuggling and enforcement network out of Tatooine, confronting Han Solo over unpaid debts before Solo leaves on the mission to destroy the Death Star.</p><h3>3 ABY - Han Solo delivered in carbonite</h3><p>Following the events on Cloud City, the bounty hunter Boba Fett brings Han Solo, frozen in carbonite, to Jabba's Palace, where the smuggler is displayed as a trophy.</p><h3>4 ABY - Death over the Great Pit of Carkoon</h3><p>During the rescue of Han Solo, Jabba captures Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa, and their allies. Aboard his sail barge above the Sarlacc's pit, Leia strangles Jabba with the chain of her enslavement, killing him as the rescue erupts into battle.</p></div>

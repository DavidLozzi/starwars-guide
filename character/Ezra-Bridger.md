    <p><p>Ezra Bridger is a human Force-sensitive from the planet Lothal who became a Jedi Padawan, con artist turned freedom fighter, and revolutionary leader in the early rebellion against the Galactic Empire. Born on Empire Day in 19 BBY to Ephraim and Mira Bridger, he was orphaned as a boy after his parents were imprisoned for broadcasting anti-Imperial messages, and survived alone on Lothal's streets. In 5 BBY he crossed paths with the rebel crew of the starship Ghost and was taken in and trained in the ways of the Force by Kanan Jarrus. Bridger's abilities grew rapidly, and his broadcast message of hope helped unite scattered rebel cells. He is best known for his central role in Star Wars Rebels, culminating in the liberation of Lothal, when he sacrificed his freedom to defeat Grand Admiral Thrawn by having purrgil pull Thrawn's flagship — with Ezra aboard — into hyperspace and out of the galaxy. Long presumed lost, he was revealed alive on the distant world of Peridea and returned to the known galaxy in the New Republic era during the Ahsoka series.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>19 BBY - Birth on Lothal (Empire Day)</h3><p>Ezra Bridger is born on Lothal to Ephraim and Mira Bridger on the very day the Galactic Empire is proclaimed, making his birthday coincide with Empire Day.</p><h3>~12 BBY - Orphaned</h3><p>His parents are arrested and imprisoned by the Empire for broadcasting messages criticizing Imperial rule, leaving the young Ezra to fend for himself as a street orphan on Lothal.</p><h3>5 BBY - Joining the Ghost crew</h3><p>Ezra encounters the rebel crew of the Ghost during a raid on Imperial cargo and joins them, beginning his training in the Force under Kanan Jarrus (events of the Star Wars Rebels pilot, "Spark of Rebellion").</p><h3>~4 BBY - Growth as a Padawan</h3><p>Ezra deepens his Jedi training, acquires and later loses his first lightsaber, and encounters figures including Ahsoka Tano and remnants of the Jedi Order as the rebel cell fights the Empire.</p><h3>~3 BBY - Facing the dark side</h3><p>Ezra explores a Sith holocron and dark-side temptations while the crew's operations expand, drawing the attention of Imperial forces including the Inquisitors.</p><h3>2 BBY - Message of hope and the Battle of Atollon</h3><p>Ezra broadcasts a message of hope across the Lothal sector that helps inspire disparate rebel cells to unite, and the growing rebellion survives Grand Admiral Thrawn's assault on Chopper Base at Atollon.</p><h3>1 BBY - Liberation of Lothal</h3><p>In the Rebels finale, Ezra helps free his homeworld and defeats Thrawn by summoning purrgil that seize Thrawn's Star Destroyer and jump into hyperspace, carrying both Ezra and Thrawn far beyond the known galaxy.</p><h3>~5 ABY - Presumed lost, search begins</h3><p>After the Battle of Endor, an epilogue shows Ezra's comrades — chiefly Sabine Wren — setting out to find him, as his fate remains unknown to the galaxy.</p><h3>~9 ABY - Found on Peridea and return (Ahsoka)</h3><p>Ezra is revealed alive on the extragalactic world of Peridea alongside Thrawn; with the help of Sabine Wren and Ahsoka Tano he escapes and returns to the known galaxy during the New Republic era.</p></div>

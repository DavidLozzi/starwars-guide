    <p><p>Phasma was a human warrior from the nuclear-ravaged world of Parnassos who rose to become a captain in the First Order and commander of its stormtrooper legions. Born into the Scyre clan, she survived Parnassos's brutal wastelands to become her people's fiercest fighter before the arrival of the crashed First Order officer Brendol Hux gave her a path offworld — a path she seized by betraying her brother Keldo and her own clan. Rising through the First Order in her distinctive chromium armor, she is known for leading the assault on Jakku, for her ruthless efficiency, and for her rivalry with the defector Finn (FN-2187). She stood among the First Order's leadership alongside General Hux and Kylo Ren, and met her end during the Crait campaign aboard the Star Dreadnought Supremacy, falling into flames after a final duel with Finn.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>6 ABY - Birth on Parnassos</h3><p>Phasma is born on the harsh, post-apocalyptic world of Parnassos, growing up as a member of the Scyre clan.</p><h3>~24 ABY - Warrior of the Scyre</h3><p>Hardened by Parnassos's deadly environment, Phasma becomes the Scyre clan's most formidable fighter and a leader of its warriors, serving alongside her brother Keldo.</p><h3>~28 ABY - Betrayal and departure from Parnassos</h3><p>When First Order General Brendol Hux crash-lands on Parnassos, Phasma helps him reach his ship in exchange for passage offworld, betraying her brother Keldo and the remnants of her clan to secure her escape.</p><h3>~28 ABY - Joining the First Order</h3><p>Rescued from Parnassos, Phasma abandons her former life, rises through the First Order to become captain of its stormtrooper corps, and adopts her signature chromium armor.</p><h3>34 ABY - Assault on Jakku and the Force Awakens</h3><p>Phasma leads the First Order attack on Jakku, later reprimands the wavering trooper FN-2187 (Finn), and is captured aboard Starkiller Base, where she is forced at gunpoint to lower the base's shields before it is destroyed.</p><h3>34 ABY - Star Wars Resistance</h3><p>Around the events of the Force Awakens, Phasma appears in operations against the Resistance, reinforcing the First Order's tightening grip on the galaxy.</p><h3>34 ABY - Death aboard the Supremacy</h3><p>During the First Order's pursuit of the Resistance, Phasma confronts Finn aboard the Star Dreadnought Supremacy. After a fierce duel the deck gives way beneath her, and she falls into the flames below, ending her life during the events of The Last Jedi.</p></div>

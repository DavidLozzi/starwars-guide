    Han Solo (Nerf Herder)     was born in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Han%20Solo?year=-32" target="_blank">32 BBY</a> and died in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Han%20Solo?year=34" target="_blank">34 ABY</a>.        
    </p>

    <p><p>Han Solo was a human smuggler from the Core World of Corellia who rose to become a general and one of the most vital leaders of the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. Orphaned into the criminal underworld of Corellia, he escaped his homeworld, briefly served as an Imperial cadet, and forged a lifelong friendship with the Wookiee Chewbacca, who swore him a life debt. Alongside his prized ship the Millennium Falcon — won from Lando Calrissian — he became famed for completing the Kessel Run in under twelve parsecs while working for gangsters such as Jabba the Hutt. Drawn into the Rebellion after agreeing to transport Luke Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi to Alderaan, he helped rescue Princess Leia Organa, aided in the destruction of the first Death Star at the Battle of Yavin, and fought through Hoth, Bespin, and the Battle of Endor. He later married Leia and fathered Ben Solo, but their son's fall to the dark side as Kylo Ren shattered the family. In 34 ABY, Han was killed by his own son aboard Starkiller Base while trying to bring him home.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>32 BBY - Birth on Corellia</h3><p>Han is born in a rough district of the Core World Corellia during the final years of the Galactic Republic, growing up as a scrumrat in the planet's criminal underworld.</p><h3>~10 BBY - Escape, the Empire, and the Kessel Run</h3><p>Han flees Corellia, briefly serves as an Imperial cadet where a recruitment officer gives him the surname "Solo," meets Chewbacca, wins the Millennium Falcon from Lando Calrissian, and completes the legendary Kessel Run in under twelve parsecs.</p><h3>0 BBY - Chartering passage on Tatooine</h3><p>A smuggler indebted to Jabba the Hutt, Han agrees to fly Luke Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and the droids to Alderaan aboard the Falcon from Mos Eisley.</p><h3>0 BBY - Rescue of Leia and the Battle of Yavin</h3><p>Drawn aboard the Death Star, Han helps rescue Princess Leia, then returns to cover Luke's trench run, enabling the destruction of the battle station at the Battle of Yavin.</p><h3>3 ABY - Hoth and Bespin</h3><p>Now committed to the Rebellion, Han fights at the Battle of Hoth, flees through an asteroid field, and is betrayed at Cloud City, where the Empire freezes him in carbonite and hands him to the bounty hunter Boba Fett.</p><h3>4 ABY - Rescue and the Battle of Endor</h3><p>Rescued from Jabba's palace by his friends, Han is promoted to general and leads the strike team that disables the second Death Star's shield generator on Endor, helping secure the Rebellion's decisive victory.</p><h3>~5 ABY - Marriage and family</h3><p>In the years after Endor, Han marries Leia Organa and they have a son, Ben Solo. Han's later estrangement from Leia follows Ben's fall to the dark side.</p><h3>34 ABY - Death on Starkiller Base</h3><p>Reunited with the Resistance, Han confronts his son — now Kylo Ren — on Starkiller Base in an attempt to bring him home. Ren kills his father, and Han falls to his death.</p></div>

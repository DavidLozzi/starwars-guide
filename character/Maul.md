    <p>
    Maul (Darth)     was born in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Maul?year=-54" target="_blank">54 BBY</a> and died in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Maul?year=-2" target="_blank">2 BBY</a>.

    <p><p>Maul, originally styled Darth Maul, was a Force-sensitive Dathomirian Zabrak male who rose from a Nightbrother of Dathomir to become the secret Sith apprentice of Darth Sidious. Trained from childhood in the dark side and an unmatched practitioner of Juyo lightsaber combat wielding a double-bladed saber, he was unleashed on Naboo, where he killed Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn before being bisected by Obi-Wan Kenobi and left for dead. He survived in madness for years until his brother Savage Opress recovered him and Mother Talzin restored his mind and gave him cybernetic legs. Consumed by vengeance against Kenobi and the Jedi, he built the Shadow Collective and seized control of Mandalore, only to be defeated and cast aside by Sidious. In the years after the Clone Wars he operated as a crime lord, leading the Crimson Dawn syndicate and later scheming as a shadowy warlord. His decades-long obsession ended on Tatooine, where he was slain in a final duel with Obi-Wan Kenobi.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>54 BBY - Birth on Dathomir</h3><p>Maul is born a Nightbrother of the Zabrak clans on Dathomir, a son of the Nightsister leader Mother Talzin.</p><h3>~54 BBY - Taken by Darth Sidious</h3><p>As a young child, Maul is given to or taken by Darth Sidious, who raises and trains him in secret as his Sith apprentice, Darth Maul.</p><h3>32 BBY - Duel on Naboo</h3><p>Sent by Sidious during the Naboo crisis, Maul kills Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn but is bisected by Obi-Wan Kenobi and falls down a reactor shaft, presumed dead.</p><h3>~32 BBY - Survival on Lotho Minor</h3><p>Sustained by the dark side and hatred, a broken Maul survives on the junk world of Lotho Minor for years, his lower body replaced by spider-like appendages as his mind fractures.</p><h3>~20 BBY - Rescue and restoration</h3><p>Savage Opress locates his brother and brings him to Mother Talzin, who restores Maul's sanity and fits him with cybernetic legs, reigniting his quest for revenge against Obi-Wan Kenobi.</p><h3>~20 BBY - The Shadow Collective and Mandalore</h3><p>Maul unites Death Watch and criminal syndicates into the Shadow Collective, seizes control of Mandalore, and kills Duchess Satine Kryze to torment Kenobi.</p><h3>~20 BBY - Betrayed by Sidious</h3><p>Darth Sidious arrives on Mandalore, kills Savage Opress, and defeats and captures Maul, unwilling to tolerate a rival.</p><h3>19 BBY - Siege of Mandalore</h3><p>Having escaped and retaken Mandalore, Maul is confronted by Ahsoka Tano and Republic forces; captured, he escapes amid the chaos of Order 66 as the Republic falls.</p><h3>~13 BBY - Shadow warlord</h3><p>During the early Imperial era Maul continues to build his hidden power base and manipulate criminal and dark-side factions from the shadows.</p><h3>~10 BBY - Crimson Dawn</h3><p>Maul secretly leads the Crimson Dawn crime syndicate, directing its operations through lieutenants such as Qi'ra during the events surrounding Han Solo's early exploits.</p><h3>2 BBY - Death on Tatooine</h3><p>Tracking Obi-Wan Kenobi to Tatooine, Maul confronts his old enemy in a brief final duel, is struck down, and dies in Kenobi's arms.</p></div>

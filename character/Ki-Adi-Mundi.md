    <p><p>Ki-Adi-Mundi was a Cerean Jedi Master from the planet Cerea, distinguished by his species' towering binary brain and elongated cranium, who served on the Jedi High Council across an extraordinary span of galactic history — from the golden age of the High Republic through the fall of the Galactic Republic. He was already a seated Council member during the crisis on Brendok and the hunt for the Sith assassin Mae roughly a century before the Clone Wars, and he was still serving on the Council when Qui-Gon Jinn presented Anakin Skywalker to the Jedi after the Invasion of Naboo, famously insisting that the Sith had been extinct for a millennium. He fought in the Battle of Geonosis that opened the Clone Wars and became a Jedi General in the Grand Army of the Republic, leading clone forces at Geonosis's second campaign, Dantooine, Quell and elsewhere, and taking part in the Council's deepening suspicion of Chancellor Palpatine. Ki-Adi-Mundi died in 19 BBY on the crystalline world of Mygeeto, shot down by the Galactic Marines he commanded when Palpatine issued Order 66 and began the Great Jedi Purge.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>Unknown date - Birth on Cerea</h3> <p>Ki-Adi-Mundi was born on Cerea, a world of the Mid Rim. Canon has never established the year; he was already an adult Jedi Master more than a century before the Clone Wars.</p> <h3>132 BBY - Serving on the Jedi High Council during the High Republic</h3> <p>Mundi sat on the Jedi High Council on Coruscant during the final century of the High Republic, taking part in the Order's response to the murders of Jedi Master Indara and others and the investigation into the fugitive Mae.</p> <h3>132 BBY - The Brendok inquiry</h3> <p>He questioned Master Sol and the Council's handling of the events surrounding the witches of Brendok, and was among those who debated how much to disclose to the Senate as the Order's public standing came under strain.</p> <h3>32 BBY - The Naboo crisis and the return of the Sith</h3> <p>As a Council member, Mundi heard Qui-Gon Jinn's report of a Sith attacker on Tatooine and declared it impossible, insisting the Sith had been extinct for a millennium. He was present when the Council reluctantly permitted Anakin Skywalker's training after Qui-Gon's death.</p> <h3>22 BBY - Battle of Geonosis</h3> <p>Mundi joined Mace Windu's task force to rescue Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker and Padmé Amidala on Geonosis, surviving the arena battle that ignited the Clone Wars.</p> <h3>22 BBY - Jedi General of the Grand Army</h3> <p>Commissioned as a Jedi General, Mundi took command of clone troopers — later the Galactic Marines under Commander Bacara — and led Republic offensives against the Separatist Alliance.</p> <h3>22–20 BBY - Campaigns of the Clone Wars</h3> <p>He fought across the galaxy during the war, including the second Geonosis campaign against Poggle the Lesser's droid foundries, actions on Dantooine and Quell, and the defense of Republic worlds against Separatist incursions.</p> <h3>19 BBY - Council suspicions of the Chancellor</h3> <p>In the war's final year Mundi joined Council debate over Palpatine's refusal to relinquish emergency powers and the growing conviction that the Sith Lord controlled the Senate.</p> <h3>19 BBY - Death on Mygeeto</h3> <p>Leading the Galactic Marines through the crystalline cityscape of Mygeeto, Mundi was cut down by Commander Bacara and his own clone troopers the moment Palpatine issued Order 66, becoming one of the first casualties of the Great Jedi Purge.</p></div>

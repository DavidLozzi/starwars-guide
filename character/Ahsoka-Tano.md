    <p><p>Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi and one of the most prominent figures spanning the fall of the Republic and the rise of the New Republic era. Discovered as a Force-sensitive child on her homeworld of Shili by Jedi Master Plo Koon, she trained at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant and, at the outbreak of the Clone Wars, was assigned by Grand Master Yoda as the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker, who nicknamed her "Snips." Over the course of the war she matured from an eager apprentice into a capable commander, but after being framed for a bombing of the Jedi Temple and cleared only after nearly being expelled, she chose to walk away from the Jedi Order. She survived Order 66, went into hiding, and later became a key organizer of the growing rebellion under the codename "Fulcrum," aiding rebel cells and confronting her fallen master, now Darth Vader. In the decades after the Empire's defeat she continued as a wandering ally of the New Republic, hunting the missing Grand Admiral Thrawn and mentoring Sabine Wren, becoming an iconic bridge between the prequel, original, and sequel eras of Star Wars.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>36 BBY - Birth on Shili</h3><p>Ahsoka Tano is born on the Togruta homeworld of Shili.</p><h3>~33 BBY - Discovered by Plo Koon</h3><p>At around three years of age, her Force sensitivity is detected and Jedi Master Plo Koon brings her to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training.</p><h3>22 BBY - Padawan to Anakin Skywalker</h3><p>At the outbreak of the Clone Wars, Yoda assigns Ahsoka as Anakin Skywalker's apprentice; she joins him at the Battle of Christophsis, earning the nickname "Snips," and helps rescue Rotta the Huttlet to secure a Republic–Hutt alliance.</p><h3>22–19 BBY - Commander in the Clone Wars</h3><p>Ahsoka serves throughout the war, growing from a headstrong Padawan into a skilled commander alongside Anakin and Captain Rex.</p><h3>19 BBY - Leaving the Jedi Order</h3><p>Framed for the bombing of the Jedi Temple hangar and nearly expelled, Ahsoka is exonerated but, disillusioned with the Order, chooses to leave the Jedi.</p><h3>19 BBY - Order 66 and the Siege of Mandalore</h3><p>Reuniting with Rex during the Siege of Mandalore, she survives Order 66 when Rex's inhibitor chip is removed, and afterward fakes her death to go into hiding.</p><h3>~18–4 BBY - Years in hiding and the rebellion</h3><p>Living in obscurity, Ahsoka gradually becomes involved in early resistance to the Empire, eventually coordinating rebel cells.</p><h3>~4–2 BBY - Fulcrum</h3><p>Operating under the codename "Fulcrum," she supplies intelligence and support to the growing rebel network alongside the crew of the Ghost.</p><h3>2 BBY - Confrontation on Malachor</h3><p>Ahsoka faces Darth Vader and discovers he is her former master Anakin; she is presumed lost in the ruined Sith temple.</p><h3>~1 BBY - The World Between Worlds</h3><p>Ezra Bridger pulls Ahsoka out of Malachor through the World Between Worlds, saving her life.</p><h3>~1 ABY - Searching for Ezra</h3><p>After the Empire's fall at Endor, Ahsoka sets out to find the missing Ezra Bridger and the vanished Grand Admiral Thrawn.</p><h3>9 ABY - The Mandalorian and Boba Fett era</h3><p>Now a wandering warrior, Ahsoka appears on Corvus and later aids Din Djarin and Grogu, continuing her hunt for Thrawn.</p><h3>9 ABY - The Ahsoka series</h3><p>Ahsoka mentors Sabine Wren, journeys to a distant galaxy, and confronts the returned Grand Admiral Thrawn, working to prevent his return threatening the New Republic.</p></div>

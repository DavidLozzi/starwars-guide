    <p><p>Poe Dameron is a human pilot and military leader, widely regarded as the best starfighter pilot in the Resistance. Born on Yavin 4 to Rebel Alliance veterans Kes Dameron and Shara Bey, he grew up idolizing his mother's flying and, as a rebellious teenager, briefly ran with the Spice Runners of Kijimi alongside Zorii Bliss before turning his life around. He joined the New Republic Defense Fleet, commanding Rapier Squadron, but grew frustrated with the Republic's inaction against the First Order and defected to General Leia Organa's Resistance. Flying his customized T-70 X-wing "Black One" under the callsign Black Leader, he became one of Leia's most trusted operatives, leading the assault that destroyed Starkiller Base. After a hard lesson in the cost of reckless heroism during the evacuation of D'Qar and the Battle of Crait, he matured into a leader, was named acting General, and ultimately co-commanded the Resistance fleet with Finn at the Battle of Exegol, helping bring down the resurrected Emperor Palpatine.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>2 ABY - Birth on Yavin 4</h3><p>Poe Dameron is born on the jungle moon of Yavin 4 to Rebel Alliance pilot Shara Bey and Pathfinder sergeant Kes Dameron, growing up on their homestead near a Force-sensitive tree gifted by Luke Skywalker.</p><h3>~18 ABY - Running with the Spice Runners of Kijimi</h3><p>As a rebellious teenager, Poe leaves home and falls in with the Spice Runners of Kijimi, forming a bond with Zorii Bliss before abandoning the criminal life to pursue a more honorable path.</p><h3>~28 ABY - Service in the New Republic</h3><p>Poe follows in his late mother's footsteps and joins the New Republic Defense Fleet, eventually rising to command Rapier Squadron.</p><h3>~32 ABY - Defection to the Resistance</h3><p>Disillusioned by the New Republic's refusal to confront the First Order's violations of the Galactic Concordance, Poe leaves the Defense Fleet and joins Leia Organa's Resistance, becoming one of her most trusted operatives.</p><h3>~34 ABY - Missions with the Resistance</h3><p>Flying under the callsign Black Leader in his T-70 X-wing Black One, Poe undertakes covert operations for the Resistance, crossing paths with figures tied to the events depicted in Star Wars Resistance.</p><h3>34 ABY - The Force Awakens</h3><p>Captured by the First Order while retrieving a map to Luke Skywalker, Poe escapes with the stormtrooper he names Finn, is reunited with his droid BB-8, and leads the successful starfighter assault that destroys Starkiller Base.</p><h3>34 ABY - The Last Jedi</h3><p>Poe's reckless heroism during the evacuation of D'Qar costs lives and gets him demoted by Leia and Vice Admiral Holdo; he learns the burden of leadership and helps the survivors escape the Battle of Crait.</p><h3>35 ABY - The Rise of Skywalker</h3><p>Named acting General by Leia, Poe leads the Resistance in confronting the resurrected Emperor Palpatine, revealing his spice-running past, and co-commands the fleet with Finn at the Battle of Exegol, ending the threat of the Final Order.</p></div>

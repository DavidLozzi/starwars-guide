    <p><p>Landonis Balthazar "Lando" Calrissian is a human smuggler, gambler, entrepreneur, and sabacc player who rose to become Baron Administrator of Cloud City on Bespin and, later, a general in the Rebel Alliance. Born on Socorro, he was the original owner of the Millennium Falcon before losing the freighter to Han Solo in a game of sabacc. Charming and self-interested but ultimately loyal, he is best known for his fraught reunion with Han at Cloud City—where he was coerced by Darth Vader into betraying his friends before turning against the Empire—and for piloting the Falcon during the Battle of Endor that destroyed the second Death Star. Decades later, having lived as a recluse after the First Order abducted his infant daughter, he answered the call to rally reinforcements for the Battle of Exegol, helping secure the Resistance's final victory over the Sith Eternal.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>~43 BBY - Birth on Socorro</h3><p>Lando Calrissian is born on the desert world of Socorro. Canon gives this date only as an approximation, derived from his stated age of 78 during the war against the First Order.</p><h3>~10 BBY - Losing the Millennium Falcon</h3><p>An established smuggler and gambler, Lando owns the Millennium Falcon and joins Han Solo, Chewbacca, and Qi'ra on the Kessel heist. He loses the Falcon to Han in a game of sabacc on Numidian Prime and parts ways to resume his own ventures.</p><h3>~5 BBY - Schemes on Lothal</h3><p>Having eased back from full-time smuggling, Lando runs assorted schemes, including a small operation tied to Lothal, and crosses paths with the Ghost crew and Ezra Bridger during the early rebellion.</p><h3>3 ABY - Betrayal and reversal at Cloud City</h3><p>As Baron Administrator of Cloud City on Bespin, Lando is forced by Darth Vader and the Empire to lure Han Solo and his companions into a trap. Regretting the betrayal, he turns against the Empire, helps rescue Leia and the others, and joins the hunt to recover the carbonite-frozen Han.</p><h3>4 ABY - General at the Battle of Endor</h3><p>Commissioned a general in the Rebel Alliance, Lando pilots the Millennium Falcon into the second Death Star and destroys its reactor core during the Battle of Endor, helping bring down the Galactic Empire.</p><h3>35 ABY - Rallying the fleet at Exegol</h3><p>Living as a recluse after the First Order stole his daughter years earlier, Lando aids the Resistance and, alongside Chewbacca, leads a massive citizens' fleet to the Battle of Exegol, contributing to the defeat of the Sith Eternal. Afterward he sets out to help Jannah find her home and family.</p></div>

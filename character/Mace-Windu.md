    <p><p>Mace Windu was a revered human Jedi Master and one of the most powerful members of the Jedi High Council during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. Born on Haruun Kal, he rose to become the Order's foremost warrior, a master of the Force and creator of the rare lightsaber combat form Vaapad, wielding a distinctive amethyst-bladed lightsaber. He served for a time as Master of the Order and, during the Clone Wars, as a senior Jedi General in the Grand Army of the Republic. A staunch defender of Jedi tradition and deeply wary of Anakin Skywalker and the growing shadow over the Republic, Windu ultimately uncovered Supreme Chancellor Palpatine as the Sith Lord Darth Sidious. He nearly defeated the Sith in single combat, but was betrayed by Anakin and killed, a turning point that sealed the fall of the Jedi Order and the rise of the Galactic Empire.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>72 BBY - Birth on Haruun Kal</h3><p>Mace Windu is born on the humid jungle world of Haruun Kal, home to the Korunnai people, and is identified as strong in the Force.</p><h3>~70 BBY - Taken into the Jedi Order</h3><p>Discovered to be Force-sensitive, the young Windu is brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant to be trained as a Jedi.</p><h3>~40 BBY - Rise to Jedi Master and the High Council</h3><p>Windu becomes one of the most accomplished Jedi of his generation, earning a seat on the Jedi High Council and developing the aggressive lightsaber form known as Vaapad.</p><h3>32 BBY - The Phantom Menace</h3><p>As a senior Council member, Windu helps question the captured Sith threat and is present when Qui-Gon Jinn presents Anakin Skywalker; he is skeptical of training the boy but consents after Qui-Gon's death.</p><h3>22 BBY - Attack of the Clones and the Battle of Geonosis</h3><p>Windu leads a Jedi strike team to rescue Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin, and Padmé on Geonosis, personally slaying the bounty hunter Jango Fett as the Clone Wars erupt.</p><h3>22–19 BBY - Jedi General in the Clone Wars</h3><p>Windu serves as a general in the Grand Army of the Republic, leading campaigns across the galaxy and serving at times as Master of the Order while balancing his duties on the Council.</p><h3>19 BBY - Revenge of the Sith: exposing Darth Sidious</h3><p>After Anakin reveals that Chancellor Palpatine is the Sith Lord Darth Sidious, Windu leads a team of Jedi Masters to arrest him and defeats him in a duel in the Chancellor's office.</p><h3>19 BBY - Death on Coruscant</h3><p>As Windu prepares to end the Sith threat, Anakin intervenes and severs his hand; Sidious then unleashes Force lightning and hurls Windu from the window to his death, a betrayal that marks Anakin's turn to the dark side.</p></div>

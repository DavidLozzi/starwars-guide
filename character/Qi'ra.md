    Qi'ra             was born in <a href="https://timeline.starwars.guide/character/Qi'ra?year=-31" target="_blank">31 BBY</a>.
    </p>

    <p><p>Qi'ra is a human woman born on Corellia who rose from the streets of Coronet City to become one of the most powerful crime lords in the galaxy. As a child she was a scrumrat in the White Worms gang under Lady Proxima, where she grew up alongside — and fell in love with — Han Solo. Separated from Han during a failed escape at the Coronet Spaceport, she was sold into slavery and ultimately came under the criminal syndicate Crimson Dawn, becoming the trusted lieutenant of Dryden Vos. Reunited briefly with Han during the Kessel Run heist, she chose ambition over their relationship, killing Vos and answering to the syndicate's true master, Maul. After Maul's death she rose to lead Crimson Dawn as "Lady Qi'ra," waging a shadow war against the Sith and the Empire before the syndicate collapsed, after which she passed her legacy to the young Cadeliah and vanished into hiding. She is known for her cunning, her survival instinct, and her tragic history with Han Solo.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>~31 BBY - Birth on Corellia</h3><p>Qi'ra is born on the shipbuilding world of Corellia and grows up in the impoverished, dangerous streets of Coronet City.</p><h3>~13 BBY - Life as a White Worms scrumrat</h3><p>She becomes a scrumrat in Lady Proxima's White Worms gang, rising to the rank of Head Girl. During this time she and fellow orphan Han Solo become close and fall in love.</p><h3>~10 BBY - Escape attempt and separation from Han</h3><p>Han and Qi'ra try to flee Corellia using a stolen vial of coaxium, but they are separated at the Coronet Spaceport. Han escapes offworld, promising to return for her, while Qi'ra is captured.</p><h3>~10 BBY - Sold into Crimson Dawn</h3><p>Passed through slavery, Qi'ra eventually enters the service of Crimson Dawn and becomes the trusted lieutenant of Dryden Vos, the syndicate's public face.</p><h3>~10 BBY - The Kessel Run and reunion with Han</h3><p>Qi'ra reunites with Han during a coaxium heist. When Vos is exposed as a traitor to the syndicate, she kills him and reveals her allegiance to Crimson Dawn's true master. She parts ways with Han, keeping his ship and choosing her own path in the underworld.</p><h3>~10 BBY - Answering to Maul</h3><p>Qi'ra makes contact with Maul, the shadowy leader of Crimson Dawn, and departs for Dathomir to join him, cementing her rise within the organization.</p><h3>~3 ABY - Leader of Crimson Dawn</h3><p>Following Maul's death, Qi'ra takes control of Crimson Dawn as its ruling "Lady," orchestrating the War of the Bounty Hunters and launching a covert campaign against the Sith and the Galactic Empire.</p><h3>~3 ABY - Collapse of Crimson Dawn and hiding</h3><p>Her scheme to strike at Emperor Palpatine unravels when the Knights of Ren turn against her, and Crimson Dawn falls apart. Qi'ra bequeaths her remaining resources to the young Cadeliah and disappears into hiding, her ultimate fate left unrecorded.</p></div>

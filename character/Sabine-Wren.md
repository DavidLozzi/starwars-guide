    <p><p>Sabine Wren is a human Mandalorian warrior, artist, and revolutionary from Clan Wren of House Vizsla, born in 21 BBY. A former cadet at the Imperial Academy on Mandalore, she defected after realizing the weapons she designed would be turned against her own people, and became "Spectre 5," the weapons and explosives expert of the Ghost crew during the early rebellion against the Galactic Empire. A gifted graffiti artist, her painted starbird came to inspire the symbol of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. She wielded the ancient Darksaber after taking it from Maul and trained with it under Kanan Jarrus despite having no notable Force sensitivity, ultimately surrendering it to Bo-Katan Kryze to unite the Mandalorian clans against Imperial rule. After the liberation of Lothal and the disappearance of Ezra Bridger and Grand Admiral Thrawn, she dedicated herself to finding her lost friend, later training as Ahsoka Tano's Padawan. In the Ahsoka era she helped track Thrawn's return and became stranded with Ahsoka in the distant galaxy of Peridea.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>21 BBY - Birth</h3><p>Sabine Wren is born into Clan Wren, a Mandalorian family aligned with House Vizsla, with ties to the ancestral homeworld of Krownest.</p><h3>~7 BBY - Imperial Academy cadet on Mandalore</h3><p>As a teenager, Sabine trains at the Imperial Academy on Mandalore, where her talents as a weapons designer are recognized.</p><h3>~6 BBY - Defection from the Empire</h3><p>Realizing that the Imperial weapons she helped develop are meant to be used against fellow Mandalorians, Sabine defects, becoming estranged from her family and a target of the Empire.</p><h3>~5 BBY - Joins the Ghost crew</h3><p>Sabine joins the rebel cell aboard the Ghost as "Spectre 5," serving as its explosives and weapons expert and painting her art across their operations.</p><h3>~4 BBY - Rise of the rebellion on Lothal</h3><p>Sabine fights alongside the crew against Imperial forces, and her starbird artwork becomes an emblem of hope for the growing rebellion.</p><h3>~3 BBY - Claiming the Darksaber</h3><p>Sabine recovers the ancient Darksaber and, though not Force-sensitive, undertakes lightsaber training with Kanan Jarrus, embracing her role as a symbol for uniting Mandalore.</p><h3>~3 BBY - Reunion with Clan Wren</h3><p>Sabine returns to Krownest and helps free her father and reconcile with her family, rallying Clan Wren against the Empire's rule of Mandalore.</p><h3>~2 BBY - Surrendering the Darksaber</h3><p>To unite the Mandalorian clans, Sabine passes the Darksaber to Bo-Katan Kryze, affirming Bo-Katan's claim to lead their people.</p><h3>~1 BBY - Liberation of Lothal</h3><p>Sabine takes part in the final battle to free Lothal, during which Ezra Bridger vanishes into hyperspace with Grand Admiral Thrawn.</p><h3>~5 ABY - The search for Ezra begins</h3><p>After the fall of the Empire, Sabine sets out with Ahsoka Tano to find the missing Ezra Bridger, later becoming Ahsoka's Padawan.</p><h3>~9 ABY - Hunt for Thrawn in the Ahsoka era</h3><p>Sabine and Ahsoka investigate rumors of Thrawn's survival. After clashing with mercenaries Baylan Skoll and Shin Hati, Sabine helps reach the distant galaxy of Peridea, reunites with Ezra, and becomes stranded there with Ahsoka.</p></div>

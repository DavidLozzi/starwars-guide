    <p><p>Cad Bane was a ruthless Duros bounty hunter from the planet Duro, widely regarded as the best and most feared bounty hunter of the Clone Wars era following the death of Jango Fett at the First Battle of Geonosis. Recognizable by his wide-brimmed hat, twin blaster pistols, and cold professionalism, he was repeatedly employed by Darth Sidious for high-risk operations, including a raid on the Jedi Temple to steal a holocron and, later, the kidnapping of Force-sensitive infants. He clashed with Jedi such as Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker, and Mace Windu, orchestrated the breakout of Ziro the Hutt, and briefly allied with figures like Aurra Sing and Darth Maul. In the war's final months he was hired to recover the enhanced clone Omega, crossing paths with Clone Force 99 and mentoring—and dueling—a young Boba Fett. Decades later, during the era of the New Republic, Bane resurfaced on Tatooine working for the Pyke Syndicate, where he was ultimately killed by his old rival Boba Fett.</p></p>

    <div class="timeline"><h3>~62 BBY - Birth on Duro</h3><p>Cad Bane is born a Duros, on or from the planet Duro; the year is derived from his apparent age rather than a directly stated canon date.</p><h3>~27 BBY - Working the underworld</h3><p>Bane establishes himself as a hardened bounty hunter, working alongside associates such as Aurra Sing years before the Clone Wars.</p><h3>22 BBY - Rise to the top</h3><p>Following Jango Fett's death at the First Battle of Geonosis, Bane becomes recognized as the galaxy's premier bounty hunter.</p><h3>~22 BBY - Holocron Heist</h3><p>Hired by Darth Sidious, Bane infiltrates the Jedi Temple, steals a holocron, and later kidnaps Force-sensitive children as part of a Separatist scheme before being captured.</p><h3>~21 BBY - Senate hostage crisis and Ziro's breakout</h3><p>Bane seizes hostages within the Galactic Senate to force the release of Ziro the Hutt, then breaks the crime lord out of prison.</p><h3>~20 BBY - Continued Clone Wars contracts</h3><p>Bane takes on numerous jobs, dueling Jedi including Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker and briefly crossing paths with Darth Maul's operations.</p><h3>~19 BBY - Hunt for Omega</h3><p>In the final year of the war and its immediate aftermath, Bane is hired to recover the young clone Omega, wounding Fennec Shand and clashing with Clone Force 99.</p><h3>~19 BBY - Mentor and rival to Boba Fett</h3><p>Bane encounters and duels a young Boba Fett, seemingly killing him before Boba survives, seeding a lasting rivalry.</p><h3>9 ABY - Return on Tatooine</h3><p>Working for the Pyke Syndicate during the Pyke conflict on Tatooine, Bane intimidates and shoots the marshal Cobb Vanth and pressures Boba Fett to abandon his claim.</p><h3>9 ABY - Death at Boba Fett's hands</h3><p>In the Battle of Mos Espa, Bane faces Boba Fett in a final showdown and is run through with a Tusken gaderffii stick, ending his life.</p></div>
